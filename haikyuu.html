Haikyuu

Haikyuu is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. It follows the story of Shoyo Hinata, a young boy determined to become a great volleyball player despite his short height. Inspired by a player known as the "Little Giant," Hinata joins his school's volleyball team and forms a dynamic duo with the team's setter, Tobio Kageyama. Together, they strive to lead their team to the nationals, facing numerous challenges and formidable opponents along the way.
