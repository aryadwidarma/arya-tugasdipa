Slam Dunk

Slam Dunk is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Takehiko Inoue. It tells the story of Hanamichi Sakuragi, a high school delinquent who joins the basketball team to impress a girl he likes. Despite his initial lack of skill, Sakuragi discovers a natural talent and a love for the sport. With the help of his teammates and coach, he strives to become a great basketball player and lead his team to the national championships.
